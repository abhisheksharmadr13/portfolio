Meta Ads Lead Generation Tier Targeting
Meta Lead Gen: 1,098+ Leads at ₹49-50/lead using Tier-Based Targeting
Structured Meta lead generation using smart tier audience segmentation. Delivered 1,098+ leads at ₹49-50 CPL on ₹1,55,932 spend across 8,81,024 impressions.
1,098+Total leads generated
₹49-50Cost per lead
₹1,55,932Total spend
What was the core challenge?
The client needed high-volume quality leads at a controlled cost. Most advertisers struggle at ₹120-300 per lead. The goal was to bring CPL below ₹60 while maintaining lead quality through smart audience structure.
What strategy was used?
Tier-based audience segmentation — Tier 1, Tier 400, and Tier 500 campaigns. Each tier had its own budget cap, creative, and optimization logic. Winning ad sets were scaled; underperformers were paused immediately based on daily CPL signals.

Campaign Results

  • Total Spend: ₹1,55,932.80
  • Impressions: 8,81,024
  • Reach: 2,83,024+
  • Leads: 1,098+
  • CPL: ₹49-50 per lead

What Worked

  • Tier-based audience segmentation
  • Daily budget caps per tier
  • CPL-based continuous optimization
  • Scale winners, pause losers fast
  • Structured testing before scaling

What was the problem?
Client had inconsistent ROAS — sometimes 2x, sometimes nothing. Budget was spread evenly without tracking which campaigns drove actual purchases. Cost per purchase was rising with no strategy to fix it.

Meta Ads Real Estate Lead Generation
Real Estate Meta: 4,623+ Leads at ₹54/lead — ₹2.5L spend
Large-scale real estate lead generation. 4,623+ qualified leads at ₹54 average CPL on ₹2,50,000+ spend — far below industry average of ₹120-300 per lead.
4,623+Leads generated
₹54Avg cost per lead
₹2,50,000+Total ad spend
Why do most real estate campaigns struggle?
Most developers fix poor results by increasing budget — but the real problem is structure. When targeting doesn't match buyer intent and creatives don't build trust, the algorithm has to guess your audience. That drives costs to ₹120-300 per lead.
What drove ₹54 CPL consistently?
Three things: (1) Targeting matched real buyer intent. (2) Creatives built aspiration and trust simultaneously. (3) Follow-up system designed to push leads toward site visits — not just form fills. Better inputs = better leads = more site visits = more sales.

Campaign Numbers

  • Total Spend: ₹2,50,000+
  • Leads: 4,623+
  • Avg CPL: ₹54
  • Industry avg: ₹120-300
  • 60-80% below market CPL

Key Success Factors

  • Intent-based audience targeting
  • Aspirational + trust-building creatives
  • Site visit-focused follow-up system
  • Algorithm-friendly campaign structure
  • No complicated funnels needed
