Reduced B2B SaaS CPL by 52% through LinkedIn restructure
A Series A SaaS company was generating leads at $280 CPL on LinkedIn — far above their $130 target. Diagnosed targeting inefficiencies and rebuilt the entire funnel.

Challenge

Campaign targeting overlapping audiences, leading to high frequency and wasted spend. Lead form quality was also poor — sales rejected 60% of leads.

Strategy

Segmented ICP tightly by company size and seniority. Introduced Matched Audiences from CRM. Built mid-funnel retargeting with case study content.

Execution

Created 3 audience tiers: cold, warm, hot. Each tier had distinct messaging and offers — awareness, demo request, free trial respectively.

Outcome

CPL reduced to $134 in 45 days. Lead quality improved — sales acceptance rose from 40% to 74%. Pipeline value delivered 3.1x ROI.

Google Search overhaul — 2.4x conversions, same budget
A financial services brand had untouched Google campaigns running on broad match keywords. A deep account audit revealed massive inefficiencies and quality score issues.
2.4xConversions

Challenge

Account had 800+ keywords with no negative keyword strategy. 34% of spend going to irrelevant search terms. Landing pages mismatched to ad intent.

Strategy

SKAG structure replaced with tightly themed ad groups. Built 400+ negative keyword list. Created dedicated landing pages per campaign theme.

Execution

8-week restructure: weeks 1–2 audit, weeks 3–5 rebuild, weeks 6–8 optimisation sprint. A/B tested landing pages using Google Optimize.

Outcome

Conversions 2.4x with same budget. Average Quality Score up from 4.1 to 6.6. Wasted spend cut 29% through negative keyword expansion.
